Mashed Potatoes

Description:

Simple, delicious mashed potatoes

Ingredients:

  • 3 large russett potatoes
  • 4tbsp Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps:

  1. Wash and peel potatoes
  2. Add potatoes to large pot and fill with enough water to thoroughly cover potatoes
  3. Bring water to boil, reduce heat to keep water at a simmer
  4. Cook until tender- easily pierced by fork
  5. Strain out the water and add potatoes back to pot
  6. With an electric mixer, beat potatoes and butter until creamy
  7. Season to taste and serve
