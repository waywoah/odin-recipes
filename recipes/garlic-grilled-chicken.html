Garlic Grilled Chicken Recipe

Description:

A rich and buttery classic grilled chicken dish. Simple and quick to make, perfect for a casual night at home. Goes great with a large variety of sides and sauces.

Ingredients:

  • Whole chicken breasts
  • 2tbsp Butter
  • 3-4 Whole cloves of garlic
  • Chicken Seasoning

Steps:

  1. Thaw full chicken breasts
  2. Heat pan to medium-high heat
  3. Add butter to pan and allow to melt
  4. Add chicken breasts to pan
  5. After 5 minutes, flip chicken breasts over
  6. Once breasts have reached a temperature of 165 degrees fahrenheit, add garlic to pan
  7. Cood for 30-60 seconds or until aromatic
  8. Remove from heat, season to taste, and serve
