Garlic Grilled Chicken Recipe

Description:

Crispy, flavorful asparagus. No bitterness here!

Ingredients:

  • Asparagus stalks
  • 2tbsp Butter
  • 1-2 Cloves of garlic, chopped fine
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Rosemary
  • Fresh lemon juice

Steps:

  1. Wash asparagus stalks
  2. Heat pan to medium-high heat
  3. Add butter to pan and allow to melt
  4. Add asparagus to pan
  5. Cook until tender, rolling asparagus when light brown crust forms
  6. Add garlic to pan
  7. Cood for 30-60 seconds or until aromatic
  8. Remove from heat, season to taste, and serve
